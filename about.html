About Us

About Us

Welcome to London Haveli, a place where culinary artistry meets the warm embrace of tradition. Established in 2024, we pride ourselves on serving authentic, flavorful dishes that celebrate the rich heritage of Indian cuisine.

Our chefs bring years of expertise and passion to the kitchen, crafting meals that are both delightful and memorable. From the vibrant spices to the freshest ingredients, every dish tells a story of love, culture, and dedication.

At London Haveli, we believe in creating experiences that go beyond just food. Our restaurant offers a cozy ambiance, exceptional service, and a commitment to making every visit unforgettable.

Working Hours

Monday - Friday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Saturday - Sunday: 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Our Mission

At London Haveli, our mission is to delight our guests with a tantalizing array of traditional and contemporary Indian dishes, prepared with passion and authenticity. We are dedicated to sourcing the finest ingredients, employing skilled chefs, and providing a welcoming atmosphere to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Our Vision

Our vision at London Haveli is to be the foremost destination for authentic Indian cuisine, where we strive to transport our guests to the vibrant streets of India through an immersive dining experience. We aim to become synonymous with exceptional flavors, warm hospitality, and an unwavering commitment to quality.
