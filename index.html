About Us

Celebrating the Flavours of India

At London Haveli, we are committed to creating a culinary journey that celebrates the rich heritage of Indian cuisine. Our team of skilled chefs crafts each dish with passion and precision, using traditional recipes passed down through generations.
Step into our restaurant and immerse yourself in a captivating ambiance that combines modern elegance with a touch of traditional charm. Our welcoming atmosphere sets the stage for an extraordinary dining experience, whether you’re enjoying a romantic evening, gathering with friends, or hosting a special event.
Our menu boasts a diverse selection of mouthwatering dishes that showcase the regional flavors of India. From aromatic curries and sizzling tandoori delights to fragrant biryanis and delectable vegetarian options, our culinary offerings cater to a variety of palates and dietary preferences.
